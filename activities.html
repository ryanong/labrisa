1. Tayrona National Park

We provide direct transport to and from the most exquisite national park in Colombia. Experience these spectacular beaches through a range of activities including hiking, camping, horseback riding, snorkeling and visiting ancient Tayrona ruins.

2. Lost City Tours “Ciudad Perdida”

Book a tour through the hostel. We work with the best, most reliable, tour companies that leave from the hostel every day.

3. Tayrona Boat Tours

Explore some of Tayronas’ pristine beaches that are only accessible by boat. This day trip includes drinks, lunch and snorkeling. Sport fishing trips can also be arranged upon request.

4. Stand Up Paddle Board Rentals

Rent one of our paddle boards and cruise around the beachfront – only two blocks from the hostel!

5. Scuba Diving/Certification

Go diving or get certified in one of the cheapest places to do so in the world.

6. Volunteer Opportunities

We work closely with 2 non-profit organizations in Santa Marta. Colombia Sin Fronteras and Fundación Mariposas Amarillas work with underserved communities in Santa Marta to help promote education through youth programs.
  • Fundación Mariposas Amarillas
  • Colombia Sin Fronteras

7. Visit Minca

The nearby town mountain town of Minca is the perfect getaway for the adventure seeker or the traveler who’s looking to relax in the cooler mountain temperatures. Minca offers an aray of activities such as downhill mountain biking, paint balling, river tours, hiking, bird watching, gorgeous waterfalls and coffee plantations.
