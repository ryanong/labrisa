From the Bus Terminal

The easiest and safest way to get here is Take a taxi from outside the terminal. Be sure and negotiate a price of 4,000 cop before getting in. Tell them the address is Calle 14 Numero 3-58 Centro Historico, right next to Plaza San Francisco.

From Taganga

If taking a taxi, be sure to negotiate a price of 7,000 cop before getting in. Tell them the address is Calle 14 Numero 3-58 Centro Historico, right next to Plaza San Francisco. Or take the bus (collectivo) for 1,000 cop and get off on the beach right next to Parque Simon Bolivar. Walk away from the beach along the left side of park and continue half a block after the park ends and the hostel will be on your right side.

From Tayrona

Pick up the Cheech n Chong bus at the park entrance and take it to the end of the line, El Mercado, Calle 11 con Carrera 11in Santa Marta. From there you can get a 3,500 cop taxi (see above for address) or walk your way back trough the market, 7 blocks towards the beach and three blocks to the left.

From Cartagena

Save yourself the hassle and ask your hostel to book a puerta a puertashuttle with either Marsol or Invertur for 50,000 cop. They will pick you up at your hostel door and drop you off at ours in an air-con minibus. The other option is the normal bus for 20,000-25,000 cop. Check with your hostel for schedules and station locations.

From Santa Marta Airport

The only real option is to take a taxi from outside the terminal. Be sure and negotiate a price of 20,000 cop before getting in. Tell them the address is Calle 14 Numero 3-58 Centro Historico, right next to Plaza San Francisco.

From Barranquilla Airport

If you like, we can arrange an airport transfer for 120,000 cop ahead of time. Otherwise, look for a taxi outside of the terminal. They will ask for 140,000 cop but try and get them down to 120,000 cop. Tell them the address is Calle 14 Numero 3-58 Centro Historico, right next to Plaza San Francisco. If you want to take the bus you will have to grab a taxi to the highway exit of Barranquilla and wait on the side of the road for the next bus to pass.
